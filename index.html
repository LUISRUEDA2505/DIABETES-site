Diabetes Information

What is Diabetes?

Diabetes is a chronic disease that affects how the body turns food into energy.

Causes

Diabetes can be caused by genetic factors, obesity, lack of physical activity, and an unhealthy diet.

Symptoms

Some symptoms include excessive thirst, frequent urination, fatigue, and blurred vision.

Prevention

It can be prevented by maintaining a healthy weight, exercising regularly, and eating a balanced diet.

Treatment

Treatment includes lifestyle changes, medication, and blood sugar level control.
